Getting Started

This app allows for fun physics simulations to be represented by Star Wars characters. You can utilize the Force to destroy baddies, escape the velocities of different planets and see ship collisions.

About Us

We are a group of sophomores from North Allegheny Intermediate High School with a joint interest in computer science. Our team wanted to create an educational app aiming to teach young children basic physics concepts. The "Star Wars: The Force Simulator" app represents our efforts to meet this objective. We hope you enjoy using our app!
